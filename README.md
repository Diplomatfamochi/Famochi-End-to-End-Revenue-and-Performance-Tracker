# 📊 Sales & Performance Analysis (Excel & Power Query)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes sales data to evaluate performance across **products, countries, and time periods** using Microsoft Excel and Power Query. The goal is to identify revenue drivers, high-demand products, underperforming markets, and opportunities for **pricing, profitability, and operational optimization**.

**Key metrics analyzed:**
- **Total Revenue Generated**  
- **Total Quantity Sold / Customer Orders**

## 🔍 Key Analyses
- **Product Performance:** Ranked products by revenue and quantity to identify high-volume, low-revenue items.  
- **Revenue vs Quantity Comparison:** Highlighted demand–profitability gaps for actionable insights.  
- **Country Performance:** Identified top-performing countries based on revenue and order count.  
- **Time-Based Analysis:** Evaluated sales trends by day and month to determine peak activity periods.

## 🛠 Tools & Skills
- **Excel:** PivotTables, VLOOKUP, IF formulas, ranking & filtering  
- **Power Query:** Data cleaning, transformation, date/time extraction  
- **Analysis Skills:** Sales performance analysis, revenue optimization, business insights

## 📈 Key Insights
- Some products show strong demand but weak revenue, indicating pricing or margin issues.  
- Office Supplies performs strongly across both revenue and volume.  
- Sales activity clusters around specific days, supporting better staffing and promotion planning.

## ✅ Outcome
Structured, dashboard-ready analysis converting raw sales data into **actionable business insights** to support strategic decision-making.

## 📝 How to Use the Dashboard

The interactive Excel dashboard is designed for management and analysts to explore sales performance quickly and make data-driven decisions. Follow these steps to use it effectively:

1. **Viewing KPIs:**  
   - At the top of the dashboard, the KPI cards display **Total Quantity Sold, Total Profit, Top Product by Revenue, and Top Country by Revenue**.  
   - These cards provide a high-level overview of overall sales performance.

2. **Analyzing Product Performance:**  
   - PivotTables show products ranked by **Revenue vs Quantity Sold**.  
   - The **High-Volume, Low-Revenue flag** identifies products with high sales volume but low revenue contribution, highlighting items for pricing or bundling strategies.

3. **Exploring Time-Based Trends:**  
   - Use the **Month** and **Weekday** slicers to filter the pivot tables and see sales trends by month or day of the week.  
   - Identify peak sales periods for operational planning and promotional timing.

4. **Filtering Interactive Slicers:**  
   - **Month:** Select one or multiple months to focus on specific periods.  
   - **Weekday:** Identify which days of the week drive the most orders.  
   - **High-Volume, Low-Revenue Performance Metric (YES/NO):** Filter to highlight products that meet the criteria for targeted optimization.  
   - Slicer selections dynamically update all pivot tables and visuals on the dashboard.

5. **Interpreting Insights:**  
   - Use the combined KPI cards, pivot tables, and slicers to detect trends, top performers, and potential gaps in sales performance.  
   - Leverage these insights to guide marketing, inventory, pricing, and operational decisions.

6. **Refreshing Data:**  
   - When new sales data is added to the source, refresh the dashboard by clicking **Data → Refresh All**.  
   - All KPIs, pivot tables, and slicers will update automatically to reflect the latest dataset.

7. **Exporting Reports:**  
   - The dashboard can be copied or exported as a PDF for executive reporting.  
   - Slicer filters remain visible in the exported report to highlight the selected view.

**Key Tip:** The dashboard is designed for simplicity and real-time decision support. Focus on slicer interactions first to explore trends, then dive into specific pivot tables for detailed analysis.
